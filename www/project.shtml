Sponsors

LaunchUMD is the crowdfunding platform for the University of Maryland. In early 2015, Observatory staff with the support of the Astronomy department, GRAD-MAP, and CMNS successfully proposed a project to raise $5,000 to acquire 2 cameras (and their control computers). The campaign was a great success, raising $8,745 or 174% of the goal. In addition, by taking advantage of educational discounts and bulk purchasing, the team was able to purchase 5 cameras.
